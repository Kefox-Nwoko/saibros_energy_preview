Who We Are

SAIBROS ENERGY SOLUTIONS LTD is a Nigerian Offshore Marine Transport and Energy Service Company.

We render essential Marine Logistics Services to the Oil and Gas industry. Our team is made up of experienced mariners and professionals in marine logistics and supply chain management.

We are the leading marine company noted for Marine Survey Support Services and the provision of shallow draft vessels and crafts for hydrographic and bathymetric surveys. We are also reputed for our expertise in breakwater surveys and other challenging marine environments.
